Morrie Helps Her Daughter with a Book Report

Morrie's daughter needs help writing a book report for her English class. She doesn't have a particular book in mind just yet, but she has the whole semester to read the book and write her report. Morrie wants to help her daughter out so that she can get the best grade possible.

Morrie's daughter presents to her the list of requirements she must complete to have a completed assignment. Morrie's daughter must read a book of any genre, and the book must be between 200 to 350 pages. In her report, Morrie's daughter must be able to identify a theme covered in the book, and write about it. She must also be able to identify a favorite character, and write about why she likes them. All of this must be tied into a summary of the book.

Wanting to help her daughter, Morrie utilizes the Overbook app to find a book that her daughter would enjoy reading and will meet the requirements for the report. She uses the search function to find a book that is between 200 to 350 pages, and is a non-fiction book. She also uses the filter function to find a book that is rated 4 stars or higher. She finds a book that she thinks her daughter would enjoy, and she sends her the link to the book.
