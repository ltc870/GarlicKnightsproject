No Internet Available

Daniel is a Navy sailor and is often underway with limited access to the internet or space. He enjoys reading magazines but wants the ability to download magazines onto his personal phone or computer so that he can have access to them regardless of space and internet availability.

Daniel needs access to digital magazines that will be available for his laptop/phone and would like a way to search specifically for digital magazines. Although OverBook has access to ebooks and magazines, there is only a way to search for digitally available resources.

Daniel submitted user feedback to the OverBook application that he would like an additional search feature. The developers added the feature and Daniel is now able to eliminate any physical-only magazines from his recommendations.
