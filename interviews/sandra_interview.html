Interview Two:

Interview Date: 1/14/2023

Interviewer: Brian J.

Interviewee: Sandra Q.

Question: What is your approximate age

Answer: I'm in my 70s.

Question: What kind of books do you enjoy reading?

I enjoy science fiction, Egyptian mythology, and African American studies.

Question: How often do you read?

Answer: I read three to five times a week, and I'm constantly either with a book or looking for a new one.

Question: Could you tell me a little bit about your education background, what you do for a career, and a little bit about your family life?

Answer: I have a bachelor's degree, and i take classes now and again, without pursuing a degree. I worked at a college but I am currently retired. I am unmarried and have one child, in their fifties.

Question: Do you have any reading applications that you use?

Answer: No, but I can only read using audiobooks now. My eyesight isn't as good as it used to be, so I try to get audiobooks either from the library or Amazon.

Question: What is your experience level with technology?

Answer: I have a cell phone, but I can't say that I use it regularly.

Question: What would you use a book recommendation app for?

Answer: I would create a list of books that I would like to read, likely from common interests, hoping to find an audiobook to read.
