Interview One: 01/13/24

Interview Date: Interviewee: Alyssa


What is your approximate age?

Answer: 37

What kind of books do you enjoy reading?

Answer: I mostly read magazines. Occassionally I will read books about cars and technology. I generally like to read about my hobbies to help with whatever I'm working on, so I like to stick with nonfiction.

How often do you read?

Answer: Intermittently, I generally will read a book over the course of a few monthes and then not pick up another book for quite awhile.

Education/job/family?

Answer: I'm a high school graduate with some college, and I am currently in the Navy. I don't have any children and I am not married.

Do you have any reading applications that you use?

Answer: I just have the Kindle app so I can keep some books and magazines on my phone.

What is your experience level with technology?

Answer: I regularly use technology, apps and games on my phone as Well as for work. I do go long periods without the internet when I'm out to sea, so I rely mostly on apps that don't require it.

What would you use this application for?

Answer: I would possibly use it to find new magazines that I haven't found or heard of. I know a lot of readers so I would probably use it for gift giving so I know a good book to recommend.
