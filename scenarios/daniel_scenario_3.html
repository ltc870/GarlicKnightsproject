Saving Lists

Daniel is a Navy sailor who enjoys intermittently reading books about cars and technology. Although he does not read often, he is constantly finding new books that he would enjoy reading when he gets the chance. Daniel tries to keep a list of these books, but either loses the list or forgets to write the list down.

Daniel would like to be able to have a place to keep these lists based on his interests. He requests this feature from the OverBook developers, who create a login system and an easy way to save books to lists the user creates. Daniel can log in and view, create, and edit his lists.
