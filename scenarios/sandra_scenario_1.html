Sandra is struggling to find a new audiobook

Sandra has always looked for a new book once she has finished with her current read. She typically heads over to the local library to return her finished book and check out a new one. She has been struggling to find books at the library lately, however, since she can only read audiobooks, and there aren't many left that are of the genres she enjoys.

Sandra also uses Amazon to source her audiobooks, but Amazon doesn't seem to give her good recommendations for new books. Since she's not exactly tech-savvy, she asks her child about how they would go about finding a new book to read.

Her child searches "book recommendation app" to which they find that Overbook is a suitable application for Sandra. It has all the necessary features, like being able to search by genre, and filtering for audiobooks, as well as being user friendly, even for someone with a low experience level in technology.
