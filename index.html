Overbook

Do you ever find yourself stuck on what book to choose next?

OverBook is the answer! It is a user-friendly resource that allows the customer to input preferred criteria and creates some suggestions for what they may want to read next.

Our product is designed for everyone

Whether you need a book recommendation to gift a loved one, if you are a teacher looking for reading requirements for the school year, or simply if you are a bibliophile, OverBook can help you out!

Our Vision!!!
