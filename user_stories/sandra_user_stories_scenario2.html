User Stories for Sandra's Scenario 2

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to find plenty of recommendations.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to have a list of all my new finds to come back to at any time.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to compare my reading lists with other people that have similar interests.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to see top books from other readers, and their reviews of those titles.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to switch my preferences for recommendations between interests whenever I'm craving a certain style.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to find books from the same or similar authors, even if they're not in the same genre.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to search through my recommendations and filter by whether an audiobook exists.

As a reader with a new interest, I want to be able to have a direct purchase link avaliable on any new recommendation.
