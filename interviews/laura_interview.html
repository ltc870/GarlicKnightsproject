Interview Four:

Interview Date: 1/14/2023

Interviewer: Kendall Ladrillono

Question: What is your age?

Answer: 32

Question: What kind of books do you enjoy reading?

Answer: I like to read fantasy, fiction, noir and science fiction.

Question: How often do you read?

Answer: Often. I love to read as much as possible.

Question: Would you mind sharing about your background? Education, career, family?

Answer: I am a college graduate. Currently working in real estate and I am not married. No children.

Question: Do you have any reading applications that you use?

Answer: Yes. I use the Kindle app, Goodreads and Audible.

Question: How often would you say you use technology?

Answer: I use it regularly. Mostly for activities such as GPS and daily applications.

Question: What would you use a book recommendation app for?

Answer: I would use it to find new books.
