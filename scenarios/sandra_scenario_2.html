Sandra has some books she wants read, but not just yet

Sandra loves to listen to her audiobooks, and she can finish one fairly quickly. As such shes always on the hunt for her next book. Recently, though, she has a bit too many books ot choose from.

Sandra has just found a new genre she enjoys and is a bit overwhelmed as to where to start. She turns to looking through a recommendation website, where she stumbles upon Overbook. As she is going through all of the recommendations, she finds that many of them pique her interest.

Sandra decides to use Overbook's Reading List feature to keep all of the next books she wants to read in one place.
