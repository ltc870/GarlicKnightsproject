Recommending New Features

Daniel is a sailor who is comfortable with technology and uses the OverBook app to find new magazines and books that he would like to read. Because he is comfortable with technology, he often would like additional features from different applications. Since OverBook is so new, the developers are interested in hearing what new users have to say, but there is no way other than having the user navigate to the webpage and fill out a survey.

Daniel does this and requests a way to send feedback to OverBook easily. The developers decided to add small buttons on multiple screens that can redirect the user to a small feedback message that can be sent directly to the developer. They also include a contact option in the navbar of their website.
