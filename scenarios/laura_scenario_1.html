Laura wants to find a resourceful book for work

Laura has been a practicing physical therapist for 7 years. Though she has great skill and is recommended as one of the best therapists in her city, she likes to keep up with the latest news and updated practices in the field.

Laura does not like to spend too much time researching on the internet for scholarly articles and research. She also finds that these are a bit of a bore to read. She wants to find a reliable book that she can easily pick up and skim through whenever she chooses.

She asks some of her classmates, who she remains in regular contact with, if there are any books they would recommend. Only one classmate responded and said that they have not picked up a physical therapy book since they were in school.

Laura decides to do a quick search on the internet and types in “book recommendation generator.” This is where she discovers Overbook.
